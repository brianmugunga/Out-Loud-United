Who We Are

Out Loud & United (OLU) is a pioneering organization dedicated to advancing the rights, resilience, and economic empowerment of Rwanda's LGBTQ+ community. We work tirelessly to create an inclusive society where every individual can live authentically and with dignity.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that diversity strengthens communities and that every person deserves equal opportunities, respect, and protection under the law.
