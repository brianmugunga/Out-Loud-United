Featured Story

October 13, 2025

Out Loud & United Officially Launches in Rwanda

Today marks a historic milestone for LGBTQ+ rights in Rwanda as Out Loud & United (OLU) officially launches its operations. The organization, founded with the mission to advance rights, resilience, and economic empowerment for Rwanda's LGBTQ+ community, held its inaugural meeting with founding members and supporters.

"This is the first step and the birth of something that will bring change to the community," said Chairperson Kwan-Lamar Blount-Hill during the launch event.
